I started at The Tropenmuseum it is a museum that focuses on the cultures and societies of the tropics, with a particular emphasis on the former Dutch colonies. The National Slavery Monument is a statue located in Oosterpark that serves as a reminder of the Netherlands' role in the transatlantic slave trade. Café Maxwell is a casual café located that serves a variety of food and drinks. Huize Frankendael is a 17th-century mansion located that is now used as a restaurant, event venue and hotel. It was a really fun ride.
